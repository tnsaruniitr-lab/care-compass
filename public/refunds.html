Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: 27 June 2026

This policy applies to care tour plans purchased from CareCompass, operated by DREAMPORT TECHNOLOGY PRIVATE LIMITED.

1. Cancellations

You may cancel your order at any time before we begin preparing your plan for a full refund. To cancel, email us via the Contact page with your order reference.

2. Refunds after work has begun

Because each plan is personalized work, once preparation has started the fee is generally non-refundable. However, if the delivered plan materially fails to meet the agreed scope, contact us within 7 days of delivery and we will review your request and, where appropriate, revise the plan or issue a partial or full refund.

3. Refund timeline

Approved refunds are processed to your original payment method within 5–7 business days. The time for the amount to reflect in your account depends on your bank or card issuer.

4. How to request a refund

Email us from the Contact page with your order reference and the reason for the request. We aim to respond within 2 business days.
