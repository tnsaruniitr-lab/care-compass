Delivery Policy

Last updated: 27 June 2026

CareCompass, operated by DREAMPORT TECHNOLOGY PRIVATE LIMITED, provides a digital service. No physical goods are shipped.

1. What you receive

After successful payment, we prepare your personalized care tour plan and deliver it online — to your registered email address and/or your CareCompass account.

2. Delivery timeline

Plans are typically delivered within 3–5 business days of a completed order and receipt of the information needed to prepare your plan. If we need more details from you, the timeline begins once those are provided.

3. Delivery confirmation

You will receive an email when your plan is ready. If you have not received your plan within the stated timeline, please contact us via the Contact page and we will assist promptly.

4. No physical shipping

As all deliverables are digital, there are no shipping charges, couriers or physical delivery addresses involved.
